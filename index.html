This is a blog post about dogs. More specifically about the twitter sensation "WeRateDogs". The account that posts pictures of cute dogs with their unconventional rating system and hilarious comments. What's better than having a twitter feed full of pictures of cute puppers.This post tries to understand dog posts and people's reactions.

  • After gathering data from the account's archive, data from an image prediction neural network and data from the twitter API "Tweepy", Assessing the data and forming a comprehensive DataFrame, Let's explore the data together to find some intresting answers.
  • Let's try to find out how this phenomenon began. By asking the question how many retweets and favorites per unit time?

  • Let's ask ourselves what is the reason behind this popularity? Was it because there was an increase in the number of tweets per month ? Is it the quantity or the quality of tweets that make people come back for more?
  • Approaching the solution should begin by counting the number of tweets per unit time, I chose the unit time to be monthly assuming it would be most convenient.

The Golden Retriever is a medium-large gun dog that was bred to retrieve shot waterfowl, such as ducks and upland game birds, during hunting and shooting parties.The name "retriever" refers to the breed's ability to retrieve shot game undamaged due to their soft mouth. Golden retrievers have an instinctive love of water, and are easy to train to basic or advanced obedience standards.
